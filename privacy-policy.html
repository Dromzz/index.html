Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2025

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Videomag ("we", "us", or "our"), operated by Florian Schafer, handles information in connection with the Videomag application, which automates the publishing of video content to TikTok via the TikTok API.

By using Videomag, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

2. Information We Access

Videomag accesses the following data through the TikTok API, solely for the purpose of publishing videos on your behalf:

  • Your TikTok account identifier (used to authenticate API requests)
  • Video files and metadata (title, description, tags) that you provide for upload

We do not collect, store, or process any personal data beyond what is strictly necessary for the video publishing function.

3. How We Use Your Information

The data accessed through the TikTok API is used exclusively to:

  • Authenticate your TikTok account and obtain the necessary permissions
  • Upload and publish video content to your TikTok account on your behalf

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with any third parties.

4. Data Storage & Retention

Videomag does not maintain its own database of user data. OAuth tokens used for TikTok API authentication are stored securely within the automation workflow environment (n8n) and are not retained beyond what is needed for active sessions.

Video files processed by the application are sourced from your own Google Drive and are not duplicated or stored by us.

5. Third-Party Services

Videomag interacts with the following third-party platforms:

  • TikTok – via the official TikTok Content Posting API. Your use of TikTok is governed by TikTok's Privacy Policy.
  • Google Drive – used as the video source. Your use of Google Drive is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

6. Security

We take appropriate technical measures to protect any data accessed during the operation of Videomag. Access to API credentials is restricted and not shared with unauthorized parties.

7. Children's Privacy

Videomag is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
